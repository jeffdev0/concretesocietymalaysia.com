CSM Past President

Concrete Society of Malaysia was formed on 15th December 1990 and officially registered as a national organization on 27th February 1993. CSM serves as unifying body of all the professionals, manufacturers, suppliers, contractors, researches and academia to serve their common interest in concrete and its related materials.

VISION

  • To be a global distinctive society for discovering and disseminating knowledge of sustainable concrete in meeting the demands of an ever-changing and challenging world

MISION

  • To discover and disseminate sustainable solutions for the betterment of mankind in an ever-changing uses of concrete and its practices
  • To provide a platform in enhancing linkages and networking with global partners towards sustainable futures

OBJECTIVES

  • To promote the advancement of concrete technology and related materials and their application.
  • To provide education and training to the industry in concrete related technology
  • To encourage local research on concrete, its constituents and other related materials used with concrete with respect to their composition, properties, manufacturing methods and techniques, test practices, distribution and management to suite local environment

MEMBERSHIP

Anyone interest in the furtherance of knowledge, research, production, marketing, training of cement, concrete and their constituents materials can become a member of the Concrete Society of Malaysia (CSM).

The difference Grades for membership of the Society are Fellows, Corporate Members, Associate Members, Student Members and Institutional Members.
